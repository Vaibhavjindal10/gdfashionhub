Frequently Asked Questions

To place an order, simply browse our products, select the items you want, and click the "Add to Cart" button. Once you have all your items, proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and make the payment.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways.

Once your order is shipped, we will send you an email with the tracking information. You can use this information to track your order on the carrier's website.

We offer a 14-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for an exchange or refund. Please ensure the items are in their original condition.

You can contact our customer service team through the "Contact Us" page on our website. We are available 24/7 to assist you with any queries or issues you may have.

The customer is at the heart of our unique business model, which includes design.
